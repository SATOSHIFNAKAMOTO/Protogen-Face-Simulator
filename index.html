Protogen Face Simulator

Welcome to the Protogen Face Simulator! Follow the instructions below to create and interact with your Protogen face:

  • Draw on the Canvas: Click on the LED matrix panels to toggle the LEDs on and off.
  • Clear the Panels: Click the Clear button to reset all the panels.
  • Export Configuration: Click the Export button to generate the Arduino code for the current configuration and copy it to your clipboard.
